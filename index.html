Kartick's Bookmark Manager

Manage PDF bookmarks easily with chapters and sections

Features

  • Open any PDF and view in built-in preview
  • Add, edit, and remove chapters and sections
  • Two-column view: bookmarks and corresponding page numbers
  • Jump to pages easily
  • Export PDF with bookmarks and open directly
  • Larger fonts for readability
